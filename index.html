About Us

Who We Are

One company, many surfaces

American Group LLC is a California-based technology company shipping a portfolio of 36 software products across mobile, enterprise SaaS, AI/ML platforms, frontier R&D, robotics, and hardware. We build the entire stack — from on-device LLMs and Wear OS apps to multi-cloud control planes and orbital edge-cloud orchestrators.

Our portfolio includes 8 mobile apps (MyHealth, Drift, Offline AI Buddy, Pocket, Card, BuddyPlay, Finoapp, UrbanNeeds), 8 enterprise platforms (ApexTrade Nexus, ArchiFlow, DocStream, GolfVision Elite, LearnSphere, MedMaster Elite, RoyalEdge Gaming OS, TitanSecure), and 7 umbrella suites containing 45 FastAPI services (AI Vertical, AudioSphere, CogniCore, DataCore, InfraForge, Productivity, XR Creator).

We also operate frontier research (World Labs, Thinking Machines Lab, StarCloud), AI research (Cognission AI), robotics (OrbitalBee Runtime), hardware (eRadar360, VendingMachine), and infra (UniConneX) — with public roadmaps for 28 future products. Built in the open, shipped under permissive licenses.
